GOED.

Packed good.


GOED. comes from the Dutch word “goed”, meaning both belongings and something good or right, reflecting a product that carries your things and improves everyday living.


A simple, multifunctional object that supports Erasmus students during their arrival and first days in their new living space. The solution is a compact product that combines storage and seating in one clear design.

Mission of the brand.


Moving to a new country as an Erasmus student is an exciting but also overwhelming experience...


What do you do with your suitcase once you’ve unpacked?


Instead of hiding it away, GOED. gives it a new function. Transform your luggage into a functional part of your space. Adding comfort, style, and ease to your temporary stay. Designed for arrival, made to improve everyday living.


From unnecessary object to functional furniture.

One object. Multiple roles.


Shoulder Bag


The top section zips off instantly, transforming into a sleek, oversized shoulder bag for your daily essentials.

Storage Bedside table


The sturdy base serves as a deep storage unit that doubles as a functional bedside table.

Adaptive Seating Cushion


Unfold the top as a premium cushion for your chair to add comfort, or secure it onto the base to create an extra cosy seat.
